FAQ

What is Watchfulness?

  • Watchfulness was created to provide recovering substance abusers with a clock tool to help track their time since sobriety.
  • It works by prompting users for a date and time selection of sobriety.The user must select a Date since sobriety. The user also has the option of selection a Time of sobreity. If the user does not know their time of sobreity, they are not required to fill in that field. If a user does provide a time, they will have a more accurate time since sobriety.

How do I use Watchfulness?

  • As a user, you can either use the app in guest mode or logged in with a user account. If logged in with a user account, your clock information will be automaticlaly saved. So, you will be able to log into your account and see your time of sobreity.

Why use Watchfulness?

  • This app is offered for free with the intention of helping recovering substance abusers track their sobriety.
  • Watchfulness is also avaible on the web for mobile devices. So users can track their sobreity from a home computer, laptop, or their phone!
